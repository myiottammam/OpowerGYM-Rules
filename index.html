Rules in English

  • Respect everyone and support other members.
  • Take care of equipment and facilities.
  • Return all equipment to its proper place and keep the gym clean.
  • Do not change or control speakers, AC, or TV settings.
  • Wear proper sportswear and athletic shoes.
  • Maintain personal hygiene.
  • Do not leave personal items at reception.
  • Return locker keys after your workout; a fee applies for lost keys.
  • The gym is not responsible for valuables; filming others without permission is prohibited.
  • Children aged 14+ may join with guardian consent.
  • Membership fees are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Smoking or chewing tobacco is prohibited.
  • The gym is not responsible for use of performance enhancers.
  • The gym is not responsible for injuries or damages during training.
  • Avoid shouting or disturbing others.
  • Security cameras are installed.
  • Violating rules may result in membership cancellation with partial refund.
  • Non-members and children are not allowed inside the gym.

Thank you for following the rules and helping us keep Opower GYM safe and enjoyable!
